Made Using Traditional Indian Methods

Our staples follow patient processes: wooden ghani oil extraction, slow-cooked jaggery, the bilona ghee method and raw unfiltered honey collection. Each method is chosen for purity, aroma and the familiar taste of home.

  • Wooden ghani extraction for mustard oil
  • Open pan jaggery made without bleaching
  • Curd-churned bilona ghee from pure cow milk
  • Raw honey kept unfiltered and unheated
